About US


Welcome to Patriot Barber Shop & Nails

               At Patriot Barber Shop & Nails, we invite you to embark on a full-on grooming adventure. Our team is dedicated to delivering the ultimate experience, combining a variety of haircut styles from classic to the latest trends with killer back massages to melt away any stress.



Your Grooming Destination

               Whether you're seeking a fresh look or indulging in serious relaxation, Patriot Barber Shop & Nails is here for you. Our talented stylists and therapists are committed to ensuring you leave feeling like a million bucks. Every hair in place, every muscle blissed out – that's our promise.



Our Mission

               We're not just about haircuts and nail care; we're about boosting your confidence and enhancing your well-being. Our mission is to make sure you strut out of our shop feeling rejuvenated, relaxed, and ready to conquer the world.
